A little bit about me.

I am a front-developer based out of Ashland, Kentucky. Before starting a career in tech, I was Registered Nurse, but quickly realized that it wasn't the career for me. I felt emotionally and physically drained and hated that I was missing so much time away from my family. I wanted a career that would give me the freedom that I craved.

Then, I stumbled across web development after searching for careers and found that I was instantly hooked! When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the creativity & freedom I was looking for in my work. Now, I've made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my creative skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.
